Novotheatre

Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Novo Theatre is a planetarium on Bijoy Sharani Avenue of Tejgaon area in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The theater opened to public on 25 September 2004. It was previously named Bhashani Novo Theatre. Address: Bijoy Sarani, Dhaka 1215, Bangladesh. Hours:10AM to 6:30PM Phone: +880 2-9138878 Established: 5 September 2004
