Baitul Mukarram National Mosque

Baitul Mukarram, also spelled as Baytul Mukarrom is the national mosque of Bangladesh. Located at the center of Dhaka, capital of Bangladesh, the mosque was completed in 1968. Address: Topkhana Road, Dhaka 1000, Bangladesh Height: 99' Capacity: 40,000 Architectural style: Islamic architecture Architect: Abdulhusein M. Thariani
