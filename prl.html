Jatiya Sangsad

The Jatiya Sangsad, often referred to simply as the Sangsad or JS and also known as the House of the Nation, is the supreme legislative body of Bangladesh. Seats: 350 Leader of the House: Sheikh Hasina (AL) Deputy Speaker: Fazle Rabbi Miah (AL) Leader of the Opposition: Rowshan Ershad (JP) Last election: 5 January 2014 Next election: 2019 or earlier Speaker: Shirin Sharmin Chaudhury (AL)
