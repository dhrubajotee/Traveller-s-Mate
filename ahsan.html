Ahsan Manzil

Ahsan Manzil was the official residential palace and seat of the Nawab of Dhaka. The building is situated at Kumartoli along the banks of the Buriganga River in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Construction was started in 1859 and was completed in 1872. Address: 2/3 Islampur Rd, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Hours: 8.30 AM - 9.30 PM. Phone: +880800-5854584 Function: Palace. Architectural style: Indo-Saracenic Revival architecture. Built: 1859-1872. Visitors: More than 3 million (in 2003)
