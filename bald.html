Baldha Garden

Baldha Garden is a botanical garden which spans 3.15 acres of land located at Wari in the old part of the city of Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh. It has a collection of 672 species of plants. Address: Narinda Rd, Dhaka 1100, Bangladesh. Hours:10:15AM-12AM. Open: All year. Created: 1909. Area: 3.15 acres (12,700 m2)
