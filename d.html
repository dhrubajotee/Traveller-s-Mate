Drik Gallery

Drik Picture Library, Bangladesh is a distinctive multimedia organisation that was established in 1989. Its major areas of expertise are advocacy and awareness campaigns, production of communication material and training. On 4th September 1989, a small group of people set up a picture agency in Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh. Named 'Drik', the Sanskrit word for 'vision', it was registered in 1994.
